Meet Matthew Brown

Matt was born in the great state of South Carolina. Around the age of 5, Matt's family moved to a small town in Kentucky called London. It was here that Matt went through the only official, accredited school(s) that he'd ever attend. After finishing school, Matt found himself working various jobs until he and his family decided to move back to South Carolina. In South Carolina, Matt attempted to pursue a career in vocational Christian ministry by attending a leadership program at a mega church in Anderson, South Carolina.

Upon completion of this program, Matt began to realize that maybe vocational ministry wasn't the path that he wanted to take. He began working, instead, in Supply Chain at Wabtec Coorporation in Duncan, South Carolina. After being there for a year, Matt received an offer to work in Supply Chain at a security company called CPI Security where he works today.

To take a bit of a rabbit trail that will help us get to where Matt is, let's discuss a hobby that he's participated in and have enjoyed since childhood; and that hobby is video games. Matt has been an avid gamer for the majority of his life. In 2020, Matt began playing lots of Grand Theft Auto Roleplay, through FiveM. In his first server, he met a lot of friends, one of which turned out to be an experienced software engineer. After leaving the server that they both were at for quite some time, Matt's software enginner friend ventured off to start his own server. Matt started by helping with testing and trying to find bugs with the server while helping set up a lot of immersive stuff for the game's players/characters. After server launch, Matt joined the admin team and ultimately the dev team where his software engineer friend would begin showing him lots of software stuff with the programming language Lua. Most of what Matt did was configurations, but that birthed a passion in coding that he never really knew that he had.

Fast forward to today, Matt has been enrolled in a program called CourseCareers which has been teaching him the ins and outs to coding to ultimately land him a job within software development. This website actually serves as his first project of many within the software world.
